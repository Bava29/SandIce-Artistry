About Us

From vision to reality, explore how we turn ideas into breathtaking sculptural art.

Our Story

Where imagination meets shoreline craftsmanship. SandIce Artistry began with a simple idea: transform natural materials into unforgettable moments. From intimate proposals to large-scale festivals, we shape stories using texture, light, and precision.

Every project starts with a sketch, evolves through detailed carving, and ends as a shared experience—photographed, celebrated, and remembered.

Our Vision
